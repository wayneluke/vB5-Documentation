## Toolbar Background
Controls the background color and image for toolbars.



- Variable Name: toolbar_background
- Data Type: background
- Default Value: 
	- units: px
	- color: #E4E6E7
	- image: url("images/css/sprite_gradients_vb.png")
	- repeat: repeat-x
	- x: 0
	- y: -100


[/segment]
